About Us

At Ace of Clubs you can access a hot meal, clothing, a laundry service, showers, healthcare, housing & welfare support and access to computers. You don’t need to be referred, just turn up and let us know how we can help.

Welcoming

Supportive

Inclusive

What We Do

Food

You’re welcome to join us for lunch Monday to Friday (including bank holidays). There is a £1 charge.

Weekdays
12:00 – 14:15

Support

Speak to a Support Worker about housing, benefits, or anything else you're struggling with. We’re here to listen and help.

Weekdays
12:00 – 14:30

Clothing

We provide clothes for everyday use or job interviews — from socks to jackets, depending on availability.

Weekdays
12:00 – 14:15

Showers

Book in advance. We have two showers with five 20min slots available each morning. Call:

Mon, Wed, Fri
09:20 – 11:50
020 7720 2811

Laundry

If you have items of clothing to wash, let us know. 24-hour turnaround. £2 per load.

Weekdays

NHS Nurse

NHS nurses are on site twice a week for medical advice and basic treatment. Call 999 in emergencies.

Mon & Thu
12:00 – 14:30

NHS Dentist

Mobile dental unit on site weekly. Call to book:

Wednesdays
10:00 – 14:30
020 7720 2811

Housing Advice

GlassDoor offer help with housing, benefits & finances. First come, first served.

Weekdays
12:00 – 14:30

Computers

Computers available for job search, emails, and tasks. Ask staff to use one.

Weekdays
12:00 – 14:30
