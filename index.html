About Us

At Ace of Clubs you can access a hot meal, clothing, a laundry service, showers, healthcare, housing & welfare support and access to computers. You don’t need to be referred, just turn up and let us know how we can help.

Welcoming

Supportive

Inclusive

  • Hot meals available Monday to Friday
  • Showers and laundry service
  • Clothing for everyday and job interviews
  • Support workers to assist with housing, benefits & welfare
  • Healthcare access: NHS nurse & dentist on-site regularly
  • Computer access for job search, emails, and essential tasks

You don’t need a referral — just turn up and let us know how we can help.
